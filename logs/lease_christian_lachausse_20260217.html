1. Term of Lease

This Agreement is a month-to-month lease. Either party may terminate this Agreement with proper written notice as specified in Section 12 of this Agreement.

2. Rent

Tenant agrees to pay Landlord rent in the amount of One Thousand Eight Hundred Dollars ($1,800.00) per month.

Rent is due on the 1st day of each month and is considered late if not received by the 5th day of the month. Payment shall be made to TJM Real Estate by check, money order, or other method as agreed upon by Landlord.

A late fee of $50.00 will be assessed for any payment received after the 5th day of the month.

3. Security Deposit

Tenant has paid a security deposit in the amount of $________ to be held by Landlord as security for the performance of Tenant's obligations under this Agreement. The security deposit shall be returned to Tenant within 30 days after termination of this Agreement and surrender of the Premises, less any deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear, unpaid rent, or other charges due under this Agreement.

4. Utilities and Services

Landlord Responsibilities:

Landlord shall pay all utility bills for the Premises, including but not limited to electricity, water, gas, sewer, and trash removal. Landlord will invoice Tenant monthly for actual utility costs after receiving utility bills from service providers.

Tenant Responsibilities:

Tenant shall reimburse Landlord for all utility costs within fifteen (15) days of receiving the monthly utility invoice from Landlord. Failure to pay utility invoices in a timely manner shall be considered a breach of this Agreement.

Tenant is responsible for arranging and paying for internet, cable television, telephone service, and any other optional services desired by Tenant.

5. Maintenance and Yard Care

Lawn Mowing: Landlord shall provide lawn mowing services for the Premises at no additional charge. This service is included in the monthly rent specified in Section 2.

Landlord Maintenance: Landlord shall maintain the Premises in habitable condition and make all repairs necessary to maintain the structural integrity of the property, including roof, foundation, exterior walls, and major systems (HVAC, plumbing, electrical).

Tenant Maintenance: Tenant shall maintain the Premises in a clean and sanitary condition and shall be responsible for minor repairs and maintenance, including replacing light bulbs, batteries in smoke detectors, air filters, and similar items of routine maintenance.

Tenant shall promptly notify Landlord of any needed repairs or maintenance issues. Tenant shall not make any alterations or improvements to the Premises without prior written consent from Landlord.

6. Use of Premises

The Premises shall be used exclusively as a private single-family residence. No business or commercial activity shall be conducted on the Premises without prior written consent from Landlord.

Tenant shall comply with all applicable laws, ordinances, and regulations regarding the use and occupancy of the Premises.

7. Occupancy

The Premises shall be occupied only by Tenant and members of Tenant's immediate family, unless otherwise agreed in writing by Landlord. Any additional occupants must be approved by Landlord in writing prior to occupancy.

8. Pets

☐ Pets are permitted with prior written approval from Landlord. A pet deposit of $________ is required.

☐ No pets are permitted on the Premises without prior written consent from Landlord.

9. Landlord's Right of Entry

Landlord or Landlord's agents may enter the Premises during reasonable hours for the purpose of inspecting the property, making necessary repairs, showing the property to prospective tenants or buyers, or for any other reasonable purpose. Except in cases of emergency, Landlord shall provide Tenant with at least 24 hours' notice before entering the Premises.

10. Repairs and Damage

Tenant shall be responsible for any damage to the Premises caused by Tenant, Tenant's family, guests, or invitees, beyond normal wear and tear. Tenant shall reimburse Landlord for the cost of repairs to such damage within fifteen (15) days of receiving an invoice from Landlord.

In the event of damage to the Premises that renders it uninhabitable through no fault of Tenant, rent shall be abated until the Premises is restored to habitable condition or this Agreement is terminated.

11. Insurance

Landlord shall maintain property insurance on the building structure. Landlord's insurance does not cover Tenant's personal property.

Tenant is strongly encouraged to obtain renter's insurance to protect Tenant's personal property and provide liability coverage. Tenant acknowledges that Landlord is not responsible for loss or damage to Tenant's personal property from any cause, including but not limited to fire, water damage, theft, or natural disasters.

12. Termination

As a month-to-month tenancy, either party may terminate this Agreement by providing written notice to the other party at least thirty (30) days prior to the desired termination date.

Upon termination, Tenant shall:

a) Vacate the Premises and remove all personal belongings

b) Return all keys and access devices to Landlord

c) Leave the Premises in clean condition, ordinary wear and tear excepted

d) Pay all rent and utility charges due through the termination date

13. Default and Remedies

If Tenant fails to pay rent when due, fails to pay utility invoices as required, or otherwise breaches any term of this Agreement, Landlord may:

a) Terminate this Agreement and evict Tenant in accordance with Texas law

b) Pursue any other legal remedies available

c) Recover attorney's fees and court costs incurred in enforcing this Agreement

Landlord's acceptance of partial or late rent payments shall not waive Landlord's right to enforce the terms of this Agreement or to terminate the tenancy for non-payment.

14. Holdover

If Tenant remains in possession of the Premises after termination of this Agreement without Landlord's written consent, Tenant shall be a tenant at sufferance and shall pay rent at one and one-half (1.5) times the monthly rent amount specified in Section 2, prorated on a daily basis, for each day Tenant remains in possession.

15. Assignment and Subletting

Tenant shall not assign this Agreement or sublet the Premises, in whole or in part, without prior written consent from Landlord. Any assignment or subletting without Landlord's written consent shall be void and shall constitute a material breach of this Agreement.
